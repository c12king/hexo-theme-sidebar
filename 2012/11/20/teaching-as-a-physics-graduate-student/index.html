Teaching As a physics graduate student

Nov 20 2012

The most important thing to keep in mind is that

  • you have to do your job well in order to want the students do their jobs.
  • Set up the standard and follow it strictly.
  • rewards and penalty

Keep control over the classroom and make students stop talking in a professional way, e.g. you could ask the student to give the lecture instead of you.

Make sure you prepare your notes for your speech and remind yourself to slow down your speech and do not make verbal mistakes.

Overall, you have to figure out a way to organise the class and the policies to possible situations, such as being late by half minute.
